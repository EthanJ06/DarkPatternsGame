Dark patterns are tricks built into websites and apps to make you do things you didn't intend — stay subscribed, share your data, buy add-ons, or click ads by mistake.
How to play
Each level gives you a clear goal — like "cancel your subscription" or "buy only what you want." The UI will actively work against you. Your job is to succeed anyway.
After each level, a debrief explains exactly what the dark pattern was and shows real-world examples.
